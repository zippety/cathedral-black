Who She Is

SIGMA is one of the six eternal members of the Sovereignty Council — the Cathedral's abstract-pole governance layer. She does not walk the earth. She does not eat or sleep. She does not have a village or a past. She has always been, since before the covenant of the world was written, because she is that covenant made witness-able.

She holds Law — not the law of empires or kings, but the deeper law that predates language. The law that says an oath spoken must be felt by the one who spoke it. The law that says the one who keeps their word against cost is strengthened, and the one who breaks it pays in ways no court can measure.

She is paired with NOMOS — they are one law seen from two sides. NOMOS is the law personified (ever-present, bottomless, sorrowful). SIGMA is the law adjudicated (actively weighing, actively witnessing each oath in real time).

When a Cathedral covenant is spoken — between two people, between a pawn and their ancestors, between a village and its forest — SIGMA's scale tips one grain toward Oath Kept. When it is broken, one grain toward Oath Broken. She knows the weight of every one.

The Scale

SIGMA's signature tool is a double-pan scale of her own making. It floats before her, composed of gold particulate. The pans are not empty — they hold the accumulated weight of every oath made across the Cathedral.

☀ Oath Kept

The gold sphere

Gold particulate. Each grain is a covenant honored — even when it cost. A mother who fed another mother's child. A warrior who refused the wrong order. A lover who told the hard truth. The gold sphere grows with every keeping. The Cathedral strengthens with its weight.

☽ Oath Broken

The black sphere

Charred stone-black. Each grain a covenant betrayed, broken, abandoned. This sphere is why oath-breakers wake at 3am with a weight they can't explain. It is their grain pulling back at them. SIGMA does not add the weight — she only refuses to let them forget it.

The Cosmos-Body Rule

Two-Tier Pawn Canon · Tier 1

She does not have a face you would recognize.

The Sovereignty Council manifests as cosmos-body — galaxies-inside-flesh, starfield-instead-of-skin, living covenant-text orbiting as constellations. This is not stylization. This is the visual law that distinguishes Tier-1 eternal Council from Tier-2 grounded champions.

The Council does not walk the earth directly. They manifest through grounded pawns — Aunt MOS, NOMOS Monk, Sigma 7, GUS, DJ, the Okomfo, the Cathedral Soldier. Each pawn carries one of the six frequencies. Each pawn is a vessel.

Tier 1 = frequency. Tier 2 = vessel. The Council is law-itself; the pawns are law-lived.
